## The problem

Client has a shopify store, and a retail location. We want to keep the prices of these two entities in sync. Doing this requires a few different things:

1. We must know when a price changes, becausing manually searching for updated pricing is wholely unreliable.
2. We have to group products physically, so we aren't running around the store placing different tags.

## The solution

Priceprint is an internal web app I developed to solve all these issues.

### 1. Create a group and scan in products.
After connecting your shopify store, you can create a new physical item group (items in the same section). You then scan the items' barcodes to load them into the group. There is a custom PWA version of the app so it can be installed seamlessly into mobile devices.

### 2. Configure your label with LabelLIVE.
Priceprint integrates with LabelLIVE for seamless printing and easy template customization. Just set up your label, and tell Priceprint where it is.

### 3. Print out your labels.
Head to Priceprint on the computer with LabelLIVE installed, right-click on your group, and choose "print". You can print all, unprinted, or only labels whose price has changed since last print.

Going over to the products tab, you can see that changing the price will cause the product to say it needs reprinted. New pricing will also be pulled from shopify automatically each day.
