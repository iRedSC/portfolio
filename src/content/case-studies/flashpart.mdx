## The problem

Client sells parts for all the tools on their storefront. This means hundreds of parts need created and managed. If you want each part to have a picture, you run into problems fast.

The issues are:
1. When dealing with many small parts that look nearly identical, photo management becomes extremely important. Mixing up photos may mean starting over.
2. It takes a significant amount of time to create and edit products, even with existing bulk tools. This is because you still have to fish for and upload each photo every time.

## The solution

Flashpart fixes these problems by creating managed part groups, which can have their pictures taken in sequence and automatically create products from that information.

### 1. Upload a parts CSV.
My client's vendors sent them parts lists that they could use to bulk upload everything except for photos.

### 2. Create a group and add related products to it.
This lets us get a group of parts together so we can take sequential pictures of them.

### 3. Open the app on mobile and start taking pictures.
The in-app camera will tell you what to take a picture of next, and move to the next one immediately once you're finished. 

### 4. Select group and upload products.
Flashpart will automatically create shopify products for selected products/groups.


## Results

- Elimates manual photo management and the potential losing of part identifcation.
- Reduces product creation time by up to 90%.
- Easy overview of what needs done, and what is complete.
